Boy Scouts of America

Be Prepared

Boy Scouts was an integral part of my personal development. Although I don't camp or fish nearly as much as I would like to anymore, I still retain a love of the outdoors and a concern for conservation. More than anything, Boy Scouts taught me the value of making a plan, and making a good plan more at that. I am very grateful to Luat Nguyen and the rest of Troop 493 for helping me on the way to becoming an Eagle Scout.

Above, you can see me and my mom after finishing my Eagle project. It was 110 degrees during this, and it was certainly not helping that we had just redone the parking lot of our local Catholic church. Planning my Eagle Scout project was a real headache, but it taught me the value of proper planning, and to always check the weather. Always remember the 5 P's: Proper Planning Prevents Poor Performance.
